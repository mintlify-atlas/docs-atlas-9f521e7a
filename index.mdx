## Get started in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to the RALQ home page and click **Regístrate** (Register). Fill in your name, email, and password to create a free account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in and open the menu">
    Sign in with your credentials. You'll land on the main menu where you can choose between **Molecular Structures**, **Virtual Labs**, and the **Periodic Table**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore in 3D or AR">
    Select any model card to view it in your browser. On a mobile device, tap the **AR** button to place the model in your physical environment using your camera.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  RALQ is designed for chemistry students at the secondary and university levels. All content is currently presented in Spanish.
</Note>
