RALQ links to two external self-assessment tools from the main menu. Both are hosted on third-party platforms and open outside RALQ. They are designed to be used after studying the virtual lab sections.

<Note>
Both tools open on external websites (Microsoft Forms and Wordwall). Make sure you have an internet connection and that your browser does not block pop-ups or redirects.
</Note>

## When to use these tools

Use these tools **after** you have studied the virtual lab sections in RALQ:

- Complete the lab instruments tour in the **Laboratorios** section first.
- Use **Evalúate** to check your comprehension of lab instrument names, uses, and safety procedures.
- Use the **Wordwall game** for a more interactive, game-based review of laboratory vocabulary.

These tools are best used as a final review step — not as a first introduction to the material.

## What the tools cover

| Tool | Platform | Topics | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| Evalúate | Microsoft Forms | Lab instrument identification, chemistry concepts | Multiple-choice questionnaire |
| Wordwall game | Wordwall | Laboratory elements vocabulary | Interactive word game |

## Accessing from the menu

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the main menu">
    Log in and navigate to `menu.php`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the tool card">
    Both tools are in the lower row of the menu grid:
    - **Evalúate** is the fifth card.
    - The **Wordwall game** is the sixth card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the activity">
    Each tool opens in a new browser tab on its respective external platform. No RALQ login is required on the external site.
  </Step>
</Steps>
