RALQ connects to a set of external resources for additional help, supplementary video content, and direct contact with the RALQ team.

## Help guide (Ayuda)

The **Ayuda** link available in the lab pages points to an interactive flip-book guide hosted on Publuu.

<Info>
The active **Ayuda** link appears in `quimica-general.php`, `analisis-instrumental.php`, `plantas-quimicas.php`, and `estructuras-mol.php`. An older URL (`https://publuu.com/flip-book/789518/1745350`) is commented out in `tabla-periodica.php`, but the current active flip-book URL across the lab pages is `https://publuu.com/flip-book/821912/1808843`.
</Info>

## Email contact

<Info>
The primary contact email for RALQ is **ralq.utsv@gmail.com**. This address is used in the Gmail compose link in the header (`mailto:ralq.utsv@gmail.com`) with a default subject of "Consulta" and the greeting "Hola, quisiera más información."
</Info>

You can reach the RALQ team by:

- Clicking the Gmail icon in the RALQ header, which opens a pre-filled compose window.
- Sending an email directly to [ralq.utsv@gmail.com](mailto:ralq.utsv@gmail.com).

<Note>
The footer of `menu.php` also lists `ralq.utsv@mail.com` and a placeholder phone number (`+123 456 789`). The canonical contact address confirmed in the header source is **ralq.utsv@gmail.com**.
</Note>
