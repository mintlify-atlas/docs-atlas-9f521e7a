This guide walks you through registering an account, logging in, and opening your first 3D model on RALQ.

## Prerequisites

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)
- For augmented reality features: a smartphone or tablet with a camera

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register an account">
    Navigate to the RALQ home page and click the **Regístrate** button in the top-right header.

    Fill in the registration form:
    - **Nombre(s)** — your first name(s)
    - **Apellido Paterno** — your paternal last name
    - **Apellido Materno** — your maternal last name
    - **Correo** — your email address
    - **Contraseña** — a secure password
    - **Confirmar Contraseña** — repeat your password to confirm

    Click **Listo** to submit. Your password is stored using PHP's `password_hash()` function — it is never saved in plain text.

    <Note>
      If any field is left empty or your email is already registered, an error message appears on the same page. Correct the highlighted fields and resubmit.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to the login page by clicking **Inicia sesión** on the home page.

    Enter your registered email and password, then click **Entrar**. On success, you are redirected to the main menu (`menu.php`).

    <Tip>
      RALQ uses PHP sessions to keep you logged in for the duration of your browser session. If you close the browser, you will need to log in again.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the main menu">
    After logging in you see the RALQ main menu with six options:

    | Option | What it does |
    |--------|-------------|
    | Estructuras Moleculares | Opens the 3D molecular structures viewer |
    | Tus Laboratorios | Opens the virtual labs hub |
    | Tabla Periódica | Opens the interactive periodic table |
    | Explora tu Laboratorio | Links to an external lab tour video |
    | Evalúate | Opens a self-assessment quiz (Microsoft Forms) |
    | Juego | Opens an interactive word game (Wordwall) |
  </Step>

  <Step title="View your first 3D model">
    Click **Estructuras Moleculares**. You see a grid of molecular model cards.

    Click any model card — for example, **Agua (H₂O)** — to load the 3D viewer. You can:

    - **Rotate**: click and drag (desktop) or swipe (mobile)
    - **Zoom**: scroll wheel (desktop) or pinch (mobile)
    - **Read details**: click the **i** button on any card to see a description of the molecule
  </Step>

  <Step title="Try augmented reality (optional)">
    On a compatible mobile device or tablet, open any model and look for the **AR** icon in the model viewer toolbar (a cube with circles around it).

    Tap it and point your camera at a flat surface. The molecule appears in your physical environment at real scale. Walk around it to view it from any angle.

    <Info>
      AR requires a browser that supports WebXR (Chrome on Android, Safari on iOS 12+). Make sure you grant camera permission when prompted.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>
